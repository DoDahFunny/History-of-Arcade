Our Mission

Welcome to History of Arcade. This website is a fan-made site created by Balin Michaw and William George as a final project. We chose this topic for our website because we both have a mutual love for classic arcade games and the feeling of playing one. We are passionate about creating websites and are happy with our product. Our central mission is to archive, preserve, and explore the history of video games created for the arcade cabinet framework.
